Rules & Instructions

How to Play:

  • Right-click on the canvas to add squiggles at that location
  • Adjust properties using the sliders below
  • Watch evolution as squiggles interact and reproduce

Squiggle Behavior:

  • Movement: Squiggles swim with worm-like motion
  • Collision: When squiggles get close, they gain hit points (colored dots)
  • Reproduction: Squiggles with 2+ hits can reproduce with similar DNA
  • Evolution: Offspring inherit mixed traits with slight mutations
  • Lifespan: Squiggles die from old age or overcrowding

Visual Indicators:

  • Colored dots: Hit count (red=1, yellow=2, green=3+)
  • DNA circle: Shows squiggle's genetic color
  • White circle: Cooldown period after collision
  • Faded appearance: Squiggle is in cooldown
