Felix Felicis

Liquid Luck — Potion Notes

Appearance:

Molten gold in colour, with large droplets leaping over the surface like goldfish—they never spill, merely splash merrily. The potion exudes a faint golden glow, reminiscent of molten metal in gentle motion.

  • Active Ingredients (canon-inspired):
    • Ashwinder egg (small quantity)
    • Powdered Occamy eggshell
    • Murtlap tentacle (thin slice)
    • Squill bulb, juiced
    • Tincture of thyme
    • Powdered common rue
  • Brewing Notes: Gentle, patient heat. Stir slowly, steady hand; perform a figure-eight wand motion on completion. The finished potion grants a short period of heightened luck — use sparingly.
  • Muggle Mocktail (safe version):
    • Apple juice 120ml
    • Ginger beer 60ml
    • Simple syrup 10–15ml (to taste)
    • Fresh lemon juice 5ml
    • Edible gold glitter for shimmer
    • Serve chilled; stir gently with a long spoon (your wand).
Prepared with care — Handle with calm precision.
